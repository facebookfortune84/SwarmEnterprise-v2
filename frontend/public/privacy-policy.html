Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 28, 2026

Welcome to SwarmEnterprise v2 ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data while you use our autonomous digital factory platform.

1. Data We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our services, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and authentication credentials.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our agents and the factory engine.
  • Payment Information: Processed securely via Stripe; we do not store full credit card details on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Provision and maintain your autonomous company instances.
  • Improve our agent intelligence and platform performance.
  • Process transactions and send service-related notifications.

3. Data Sovereignty

Unlike traditional SaaS, SwarmEnterprise v2 is designed for self-hosting. When you deploy a "Company in a Box," you retain full ownership and control over the data within that instance.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including end-to-end encryption for sensitive metadata and secure vaulting for API keys.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@realms2riches.com.
